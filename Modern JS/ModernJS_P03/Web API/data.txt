"Everything Everywhere All at Once" is a 2022 American science fiction film directed by Dan Kwan and Daniel Scheinert. 
The movie follows a woman named Dolores who discovers that she has the ability to access parallel universes, 
leading her on a journey to save her family and ultimately the multiverse itself. The film stars Michelle Yeoh, 
Stephanie Hsu, and Jamie Lee Curtis, among others. It has been praised for its creative visuals, imaginative storytelling, 
and impressive performances.
